Fun Fact


I have a very unique name. My first name Saraiel came from a person in the Bible,
Sarai (the original name of Sarah), the wife of Abraham (originally Abram).
The EL at the end of Saraiel are actually the initials of my parents, Eduardo and Luningning.
El can also signify "The One" and Sarai means "Princess."

Sanghaya is a Filipino word that has a lot of meanings such as high honor, dignity, integrity,
beauty, great personal charm (esp. of women), love, fragrance, etc.

Together, my name means The Princess of High Honor, Dignity, and Integrity.
I aim to embody such virtues of high honor, dignity, and integrity in all I do.
But of course, you can just call me Sara for short.
