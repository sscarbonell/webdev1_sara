Chicken Adobo

Chicken slices cooked in soy sauce and vinegar with garlic.
This is a delicious Filipino chicken dish that you can eat for lunch
with warm white rice.





Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken
  • 3 pieces dried bay leaves
  • 4 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon whole peppercorn


Steps

  1. Combine chicken, soy sauce, and garlic in a large bowl. Mix well.
  2. Marinate the chicken for at least 1 hour. Note: the longer the time, the better.
  3. Heat a cooking pot. Pour cooking oil.
  4. Remove from the wok and set aside.
  5. Add the mixed vegetables to the wok and stir-fry for a few minutes until they become crisp-tender.
  6. In a bowl, whisk together the chicken broth, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and dissolved cornstarch.
  7. When the oil is hot enough, pan-fry the marinated chicken for 2 minutes per side.
  8. Pour in the remaining marinade, including garlic. Add water. Bring to a boil
  9. Add dried bay leaves and whole peppercorn. Simmer for 30 minutes or until the chicken gets tender
  10. Add vinegar. Stir and cook for 10 minutes.
  11. Put in the sugar, and salt. Stir and turn the heat off.Serve hot. Share and Enjoy!

Serving Suggestion: This delicious Chicken Adobo recipe serves 2-4 people
and is best enjoyed with family or friends. You can also customize it by adding chili for an extra spicy kick!
