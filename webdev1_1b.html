Chopsuey

A delightful stir-fried dish that combines a variety of fresh vegetables
and your choice of protein in a seasoned sauce.



Ingredients

  • 2 cups of mixed vegetables (e.g. bell peppers, carrots, cabbage, snow peas, baby corn, etc.)
  • 200g of your choice of protein (chicken, pork, shrimp, or tofu)
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of cornstarch dissolved in 2 tablespoons of water
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Steamed rice for serving


Steps

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat.
  2. Saut√© the minced garlic and sliced onion until they become fragrant.
  3. Add your choice of protein (chicken, pork, shrimp, or tofu) and
    stir-fry until it's cooked through and lightly browned.
  4. Remove from the wok and set aside.
  5. Add the mixed vegetables to the wok and stir-fry for a few minutes until they become crisp-tender.
  6. In a bowl, whisk together the chicken broth, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and dissolved cornstarch.
  7. Pour this sauce into the wok.
  8. Return the cooked protein to the wok and stir everything together.
  9. Simmer for a couple of minutes until the sauce thickens.
  10. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  11. Serve hot over steamed rice.


Serving Suggestion: This delicious Chopsuey recipe serves 2-4 people
and is best enjoyed with family or friends. You can also customize it by adding chili for an extra spicy kick!
